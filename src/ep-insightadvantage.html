Because not all business is good business

The real-time business quality filter

Designed to boost the underwriting profitability of insurers, but suitable for all companies which need to assess new business applications

Most new business processes treat applications in isolation. They take in details and come to a decision based on the merits of that case alone.

Insight Advantage is a clever piece of technology which sits in your new business process and remembers past applications. If a subsequent application is made by the same person, it makes available the previous data to the case. This allows decision-making to be based upon the whole picture.

Build a bigger picture

Insight Advantage begins working from the very first interactions with a customer - even if they are 'hidden' behind an aggregator.

By putting together an individual's pattern of quoting, even across years, Insight Advantage lets insurers better gauge their acceptability as a risk.

Detect poor quality cases

Insight Advantage provides actuaries and underwriters with additional factors on which they can base their pricing and decision making.

In real time, insurers can make better decisions:

  • whether to quote,
  • apply a loading,
  • require extra u/w checks.
