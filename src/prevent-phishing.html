How to prevent phishing?

Account Shield - the simple and effective way to protect your customer accounts


Phishing and other such scams are the plague of modern online life. Fraudsters have no qualms about trapping the unwary - however elderly or impaired - into handing over their login credentials.

There are measures you can take to protect your customers

Measures such as . . .

school Education
Explain how you will and won't communicate with them. Provide advice on how they can tell whether an email or website is genuine
device_hub Technical
There are measures that can be taken to make it harder for scammers to steal or copy your website imagery. And ways to scan for domains which may have been setup with a similar name in order to deceive your customers. This is a specialist area - one where our sister company Echo Protect can help
However, no measures are completely effective
you must assume that some account credentials will always be compromised

It is important to put in place protection in the event that scammers do obtain the credentials of your customers.

Account Shield protects your customers by silently adding an additional layer of device recognition to your website. Now, even with the correct login details, fraudsters won't get in - Account Shiekd will block access and alert you and the account holder.
